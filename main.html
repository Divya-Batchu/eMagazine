Sree Vidyanikethan is the college – the one Engineering College – that comes to people’s minds at the mention of Engineering in Andhra Pradesh, under the widely regarded group of institutions catering the best of education in India. Known for its vision and benevolence, Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College designs the study programmes to meet tomorrow’s industry demands. It gives me great pride every year to see our graduates invariably preferred by most of the employers. The magnitude of campus placements is undoubtedly the yardstick of our quality education delivery here.

SVEC owns state-of-the-art infrastructure, world-class research facilities, best faculty and active student groups. This will make us to help the students on the promising platform in terms of professional education. SVEC always finds its way in the ‘Top 10’ list of engineering colleges in the country.
